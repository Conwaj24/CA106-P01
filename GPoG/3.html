Modern Day:

Today, the Great Pyramid of Giza is one of the world's most renowed historical monuments, for a multitude of reasons. Not only is it the only surviving wonder to date, but it is also a big source of discoveries especially within the scientific world.

The Great Pyramid of Giza has lead to the discovery of structures being able to focus electromagnetic energy, This could lead to the development of efficient solar cells within the near future as physicists investigate further.

The Pyramid also boasts several million visitors from all around the world, yearly, who all wish to view and explore the only remaining wonder to date, thus making it one of the most visited attractions of all times.
