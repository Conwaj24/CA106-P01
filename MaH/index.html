Introduction:

The Mausoleum at Halicarnassus was an above-ground tomb built by and for the Persian satrapThe govenor of a province in ancient Persia.
-Merriam Webster Mausolus in the lavish city founded under his and his father's conquest. While originally named after Mausolus, the term Mausoleum has since come to mean any above ground tomb.
