Cryptocurrencies & Blockchain

--

For me personally, it's less about the financial aspect of Crypto, and more about the technology.

I strongly believe that over time, blockchain will slowly overrule the standard ledger we know today.

Having a public ledger, such as in the case of Bitcoin, will help the world from problems such as corruption.

I've worked on a series of projects, and are involved in the managment of several ICOs. You probably won't find someone else my age as interested in this field as myself.

Safety

--

Safety online is just as important as physical safety to me.

At no point should you ever feel threatened or scared.

The internet should be a place where one can freely roam without any consequences and without any fears.

We should stride to fight cyber crime, and turn the cyber world into a cyber dystopia.

Privacy

--

We live in a time where our digital identities now have a price to someone.

There are certain indvidiuals with malicious intentions, who wish to breach our privacy and sell our digital identity.

Privacy is extremely important to me. Seeing who has access to your data, and why is key to securing your digital identity.

Prioritize your privacy over anything else before someone else prioritizes it for you without your consent.
