What is our idea?

Our idea is illustrating the history of each wonder under the following headings:

  • birth (construction, inspiration)
  • life (historical accounts, purpose)
  • death (modern day where destruction not applicable e.g. pyramids)
  • undecided (sources, modern day, image gallery)

Our chosen wonders:

Lighthouse of Alexandria

Hanging Gardens of Babylon

The Great Pyramid of Giza
