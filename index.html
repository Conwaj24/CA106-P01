What are the Seven Wonders?

These ancient structures were marvels of an age of progress and exploration. they have been endlessly studied and celebrated by those of multiple professions.

There is great history surrounding these wonders with regards to scholars, writers and artists for many years. These pieces of architecture were proof of human achievement in the most spectacular way, showing the power and influence of each empire that governed them.

Mostly built by the Mediterranean and Middle Eastern empires, with crude tools and manual labour, there is abounding proof of the style and ingenuity of thes vast emprires. Today sadly, all but one of these ancient wonders have vanished.

The Wonders

  • Lighthouse of Alexandria
  • Hanging Gardens of Babylon
  • The Great Pyramid of Giza
  • The Mausoleum at Helicarnassus
  • The Colossus of Rhodes
