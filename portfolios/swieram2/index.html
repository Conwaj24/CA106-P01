About Me

My name is Maciej Zamoyski Swierad, I was born in a small town in Poland called Nowy Sacz. From a young age I've had a massive interest in computing (thanks to my brother and uncles whom all work in the field)
I moved to Ireland when I was 4, and have lived here ever since. Most of my summers I spent in DCU in CTYI (Centre For Talented Youth Ireland) Programme doing courses ranging from Cutting Edge Science to Law.
After taking Law in my last year of CTYI, my plans of being a computer scientist shifted and I wanted to be a barrister, however in the end I ended up pursuing computer science anyway. One day I hope to master in the Bar of Ireland.
I've built and sold websites, I've participated in competitive programming and my plan after my degree is to work in Cyber Security, however my passion lies in authorship, and I hope to one day publish a book
